The Bespoke Process

Dapper uses the same technique that the tailors from Savile Row and all around the world have been using for hundreds of years. From the cutting, fitting to recutting, adjusting and sculpting the whole thing only using hands, there's a tremendous amount of work that goes in a bespoke garment. It's a more human process rather than a mechanical one, where you build a relationship with your tailor and quite a few people are directly involved in the making of your suit.
